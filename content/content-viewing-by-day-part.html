Viewing By Day Part

The Viewing by Day Part graph shows the local time viewers consumed content, grouped by the time of day when the view occurred. If a view stretches across multiple day parts, that view is counted in each of the day parts it spans.

Actions

Select one of the following to display data in the graph:

  • Video Views - Estimated number of video views
  • Audience Size - Estimated number of viewers
Video views and audience size values are estimated based on random viewer sampling. For viewer populations of more than 30,000, statistics are projected from a random sample of viewer behavior. Using 30,000 viewers ensures a margin of error of less than 1%.
